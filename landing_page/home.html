FAQs

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, you can try all features for 7 days without any charge.

Which platforms are supported?

Our app is available on iOS and Android devices.

How can I share my artwork with the community?

You can share your artwork directly from the app by selecting the 'Share' option and choosing your preferred platform.

What types of brushes are available?

Ink Muse offers a variety of brushes including pencils, pens, markers, and special effect brushes to suit all your artistic needs.

Is there customer support available?

Yes, we offer 24/7 customer support. You can reach us through the 'Contact Us' section in the app or via email at support@inkmuse.com.

Can I export my artwork?

Yes, you can export your artwork in various formats such as JPEG, PNG, and PDF.

Does Ink Muse offer any tutorials?

Yes, Ink Muse provides in-app tutorials and guides to help you get started and make the most out of the app's features.
